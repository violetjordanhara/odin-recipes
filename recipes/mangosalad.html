mango salad recipe

Description

If you have unripe mangoes and fish sauce, make this!

ingredients

for salad dressing

  • 3 tbsp fish sauce OR .25 cup soy sauce
  • .25 cup lime juice
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 to 2 tsp thai chile sauce OR .5 tsp dried chile flakes

for salad

  • .25 cup dry unsweetened coconut
  • 2 unripe green mangoes
  • 4 thinly sliced spring onions
  • 1 cup cooked, coarsely chopped chicken OR fried tofu
  • 1 small red chile
  • .25 cup peanuts or cashews
  • .3 cup small fresh basil

steps

  1. mix ingredients for salad dressing
  2. dry fry coconut
  3. peel and grate mangoes
  4. add veggies and salad dressing
